Citadel Outlets The Castle Outlet Malls Closest to LA Downtown Citadel Outlets is the outlet mall closest to downtown Los Angeles, located just a 20-minute drive from Downtown LA. For those not planning on driving, they offer free shuttle service between downtown and Citadel, departing at scheduled times with set routes. Additional services include luggage storage and currency exchange facilities, plus free parking. While you won't find top-tier luxury brands here, common brands like Coach, Michael Kors, Kate Spade, and others are readily available. Named for its castle-like appearance, Citadel is of medium size but features unique architecture with winged lions as its emblem, creating an exotic atmosphere. While window shopping, you can also enjoy the area's cultural scenery. Foodies needn't worry either—there's a food court built in 1990, offering everything from convenient fast food to delicious healthy fat-free yogurt, ensuring you never leave hungry! Camarillo Premium Outlets Largest Outlet Mall in Northwest Los Angeles Camarillo is located northwest of Los Angeles city center, requiring at least an hour from downtown, but it's closer than desert outlets. With over 120 brands and more than 160 stores, deals ranging from 30-50% off are quite attractive. Camarillo Outlets consists of three sections and features luxury department store outlet locations: Barneys New York Outlets, Last Call by Neiman Marcus, and Saks Fifth Avenue Off 5th. These stores offer large quantities of discounted premium brand past-season inventory, making it an excellent place to purchase high-end clothing and handbags. Popular American brands such as Tory Burch, Coach, Guess, J.Crew, Polo Ralph Lauren, North Face, Timberland, etc. are all available here with relatively low tax rates. Desert Hills Premium Outlets The largest outlet mall in Southern California/Palm Springs area One of the largest outlet malls in all of California or even the entire western United States, earning the title "shopping paradise in the desert." Located in eastern Los Angeles area, about 2 hours drive from downtown, with views of desert scenery in western America along the way. All major brands are here, ranging from world top-tier brands like Burberry, Gucci, Dior, Dolce&Gabbana, Elie Tahari, Giorgio Armani, Lacoste, to American domestic brands such as Gap, Levis, Coach - everything you can find is available. However, affordable youth-oriented brands like Forever 21, H&M, A&F are not here. Nearby there are famous tourist attractions Joshua Tree National Park and Morongo Casino Resort and Spa. Energetic visitors might also want to explore the surrounding areas. Desert Hills encourages group visits; if you have 15 or more people, you can register on their official website so that when your team arrives at the outlets, you will receive a free VIP Coupon Book. Additionally, directly registering for the VIP Shopper Club can also get you a voucher for a Free VIP Coupon Book, but it is recommended to register just before your trip so you can get fresh discount coupons! Ontario Mills - California's Largest Indoor Outlet Mall Considered one of the largest indoor outlet malls in North America, with 10 gates commonly called "Ten Gates," Ontario is also known as USC's largest outlet area. The biggest advantage of Ontario is being fully indoors, protecting you from wind and sun exposure, regardless of extreme cold or heat. Most importantly - there's even a movie theater! For gentlemen tired of shopping, this makes it an excellent place to relax. While Ontario doesn't have Gucci or Crabtree-Elysees, it features brands everyone loves such as Nordstrom, Coach, Timberland, plus light luxury and affordable brands favored by young people. Beyond the food court, there are many delicious small food shops, making it a great option when you're done shopping for something to eat.
