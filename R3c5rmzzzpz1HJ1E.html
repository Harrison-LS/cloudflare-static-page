With such an abundance of restaurants and cafes, there are simply too many to count, and almost every week sees new establishments opening their doors! ③ No filters needed The natural sunlight and endless golden moments brought by good weather allow you to uninstall filter apps from your phone. Actually, I'm someone who loves the sea more than mountains. Runyon Canyon in Hollywood Hills and Griffith Observatory are favorite hiking routes for LA locals (especially celebrities). You can breathe fresh air while hiking and enjoy panoramic views of the spectacular city below. These creative graffiti pieces scattered throughout LA have also become one of the favorite photo backdrops for bloggers. ⑥ Visit Hollywood Filming Locations In LA you can experience more than just famous studio tours.
