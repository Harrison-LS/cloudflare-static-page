Grauman's Chinese Theatre Sidney Grauman, known as "Mr. Hollywood," built his dream Chinese Theatre in 1927 on a prime location along Hollywood Boulevard. This theater with its Oriental artistic style has hosted multiple Academy Award ceremonies. The plaza outside is filled with handprints and footprints of film superstars, including iconic Hollywood stars like Marilyn Monroe and Sylvester Stallone, as well as Chinese celebrities such as Jackie Chan (Cheng Long), Zhao Wei, and Huang Xiaoming. Inside the theater is a giant IMAX cinema that regularly screens popular Hollywood films. Though tickets are more expensive than regular theaters, the viewing quality remains at "Hollywood" standards.
