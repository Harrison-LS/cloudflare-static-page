Living in sunny Los Angeles, how can you miss the experience of enjoying sunshine by the seaside, feeling the ocean breeze, and savoring delicious food? Eating alone as a lone wolf? Friends visiting Shanji Village - where to take them for a meal? Parents coming to town - still wondering where to eat that's comfortable with a nice environment? Of course, go relax by the beach! Today, our little team has compiled LA's ocean-view restaurant guide for 2018! Sorted from low to high price. 1. Whiskey Red's Restaurant $$ Rosemary roasted chicken, fresh oysters of the season, limited-time champagne brunch every Sunday from 9am-2pm, whiskey chicken wings... Escape the city's hustle and bustle and enjoy a hearty brunch on a weekend morning! This American small shop has a rich menu that can definitely satisfy your picky taste buds. With its relaxed and casual dining atmosphere, this restaurant is included in LA's Top 17 Scenic Restaurants. Address: 13813 Fiji Way, Marina Del Rey, CA 90292 2. ONYX Rooftop Bar This rooftop bar is located at the Shangri-La Hotel. As soon as you sit down, you can take in views of the Santa Monica Beach from above. Friends who've tried it are all choosing whiskey and cocktails. After 10 PM there are many people and service is slower. Suggest enjoying a drink at sunset or after work to relax, then moving on to another venue~ Address: 1019, 1301 Ocean Ave, Santa Monica, CA 90401 3. The Venice Whaler Seafood🦐, beer🍺, cocktails🍸. Located by Venice Beach, this American restaurant is a favorite choice for many LA locals. The reputation of their various mixed drinks, beers, and white wines surpasses the seafood. Rated as one of the top four restaurants in Los Angeles area for best Bloody Marys. Address: 10 Washington Blvd, Venice, CA 90292 4. Duke's Malibu $$ A scenic yet low-key Hawaiian-style small spot with a relaxed summer atmosphere near Malibu Beach, perfect for catching up with friends over the weekend. ⚠️ Note: Best to make reservations in advance. This restaurant requires you to accept $6 valet parking service (not including tip), so please choose your parking location carefully. Address: 21150 Pacific Coast Hwy, Malibu, CA 90265 5. Malibu Farm Restaurant $$$ Malibu Farm is divided into a restaurant and cafe section, located at the entrance and far end of the marina respectively. Claimed to have the best ocean view in Malibu Beach, this restaurant focuses on healthy organic ingredients from farm-to-table. It gets quite crowded at night, so I recommend visiting during the day. The Cafe offers an even better extended coastal scenery! Recommended dishes: salmon salad and Vegan Coconut Bowl. Address: 23000 Pacific Coast Hwy, Malibu, CA 90265 6. The Loft $$$ Delicious food! Ocean view! Music! The Loft is a low-key American restaurant located within the Montage Hotel. Since fewer people know about it, the atmosphere is elegant. During meals there are piano performances bringing relaxed and pleasant music. It's one of the niche ocean-view restaurants in Los Angeles worth trying. Address: 30801 Coast Hwy, Laguna Beach, CA 92651 7. Water Grill $$$ One of Santa Monica's top ten restaurants, this place boasts an excellent location right next to Santa Monica Beach.
