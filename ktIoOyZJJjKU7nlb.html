Los Angeles

Brief

The most iconic scene from "La La Land" is undoubtedly the playful waltz between the lead couple at the Griffith Observatory in Los Angeles.

Content

The 2016 Hollywood film "La La Land" can be called a "myth" among Hollywood musical romance films. Not only did it win all six Academy Awards, but also secured over 100 world-class film awards globally, drawing worldwide attention. The film tells the story of a jazz pianist (Sebastian) and an aspiring actress (Mia) pursuing their dreams together while gradually developing feelings for each other. Although the ending doesn't seem as perfect as imagined, the contradictions between life and love and the steadfastness toward dreams are interpreted by the director so realistically and purely. The most iconic scene in the film is undoubtedly when the lead couple performs a playful waltz at Griffith Observatory in Los Angeles, creating beautiful imagery for audiences. With the beautifully illuminated Los Angeles cityscape behind them, this moment brings the film into its first climax, making this filming location a popular "pilgrimage" site for tourists. Where is the famous La La Land photo wall? This iconic scene was filmed at Cathy's Corner, a hiking trail within Griffith Park in Los Angeles. Not only can you recreate scenes from the film here, but you can also conveniently visit two of Los Angeles' landmark attractions: the Hollywood Sign and Griffith Observatory. Two attractions are within walking distance at Cathy's Corner. LA LA LAND on Melrose Avenue On Los Angeles' Melrose Ave, there is even a LA LA LAND fan wall. This wall became a must-visit spot for tourists during the movie's popularity peak. Although the wall has recently been changed in theme, it will be displayed again at Sanlitun Orange Hall from December 8, 2017 to January 1, 2018. Don't miss this best opportunity to take photos with LA LA LAND! Melrose Avenue Melrose Avenue is a shopping street where young people gather in Los Angeles. There are many local specialty boutique shops here selling unique and rare items and avant-garde clothing stores. Besides that, big brand stores, art galleries, vintage boutiques, and upscale restaurants are everywhere. When you get tired of shopping, finding a restaurant is absolutely not difficult. In short, everything you want is here. Take Your Own LA LA LAND Photos Imitate the poses from the film Whether it's dynamic dance poses or passionate embrace photos, imitating the lead couple's positions from the show is the best way to recreate the original romance. Reenact scenes from the drama If you can learn a few dance steps from the show, whether recording a short video or capturing dance moments, it will definitely add color to your LA LA LAND photos. Explore more filming locations of LA LA LAND Griffith Observatory by odin and kitty The scene of the leads exploring the starry sky at the observatory was filmed at Griffith Observatory on top of the hill. Here is not only an astronomy museum, but also an excellent spot to take photos with the Hollywood sign and overlook the entire Los Angeles area. At the open-air platform at the top, you can see the orderly streets, neat city buildings, and skyscrapers of the downtown area all clearly. Watching a red sunset sink into the sea from the platform at dusk has its own unique charm. Griffith Observatory • Located on a mountain top northwest of Los Angeles, you can see the entire city spread out below. • Displays astronomical knowledge about planets and the universe; the dome cinema at the top is built to resemble the sky, allowing 360° viewing of films showing celestial evolution. Naked-eye 3D technology brings everything in the universe within reach. • The open-air platform also offers a view overlooking Los Angeles city - orderly streets, neat buildings, and downtown skyscrapers are all clearly visible. • After nightfall, the observatory is free to enter, where you can see stars twinkling above and outlines of the moon's surface. Hollywood Sign The famous Hollywood sign stands atop Griffith Park's mountain peak. While the sign itself is no longer accessible to visitors, several hiking trails offer many excellent spots for taking photos with it. Tourists can hike the paths like locals, or visit nearby attractions such as the Los Angeles Zoo, planetarium, and open-air Greek Theatre. Hollywood Sign • When thinking of Hollywood, one naturally recalls the massive HOLLYWOOD letters standing tall on the mountain top. • The landmark's original full name was HOLLYWOOD LAND, used as an advertising slogan for Hollywood film studios. • After 1960, the letters LAND were removed, creating the now-familiar HOLLYWOOD sign. • You can only take photos with it from nearby Griffith Observatory or within the urban area. The Light House Cafe The Light House Cafe is located in Hermosa Beach. Since May 1949, it has been a venue for jazz music performances. Legendary jazz musicians Miles Davis, Chet Baker, and Art Blakey have all performed here. In the film, this was also the jazz bar where the male and female leads went on their first date. Angels Flight The train slowly ascends as they gradually approach—a tender kiss at this "Angels Flight" in downtown Los Angeles is incredibly romantic. This railway, listed on the U.S. National Register of Historic Places, is only 99 meters long and is the world's shortest cable railway. Riding Angels Flight allows you to feel the strong retro charm of downtown Los Angeles. However, regretfully, due to safety considerations, Angels Flight was permanently closed in 2013. However, the producer managed to secure permission for one day of filming on the Angel Railway. Angel Railway The Angel Railway is a filming location for the movie "La La Land," where Mia and Sebastian had romantic dates and shared sweet kisses in wooden carriages. The Angel Railway has been operating since 1901, making it the world's shortest railway. Tourists can ride the Angel Railway to experience this brief yet graceful journey with its vintage red-and-black livery and wooden carriages. Hermosa Beach As one of Los Angeles' most beautiful beaches, Hermosa Pier stretches along the coastline, offering a favored spot for fishing and sunset viewing. Hermosa Beach is close to the Lighthouse Cafe and can be explored together on foot. Hermosa Beach Hermosa Beach was also a filming location for the movie "La La Land." After leaving the Lighthouse Cafe, Sebastian strolled along the pier and sang "City of Stars." Nearby is the Hermosa Pier, a romantic destination for couples. The restaurants in the area offer beautiful ocean views, where one can enjoy a romantic candlelit dinner while watching the sunset.
