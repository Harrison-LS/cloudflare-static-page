What Luxury Experiences Are Available in Los Angeles? Whether it's checking into a world-class hotel to enjoy comfortable spa treatments or chartering a yacht to admire stunning ocean views—every luxurious lifestyle you can imagine comes true here! Staying in "Mansions": New Luxury Hotels in Los Angeles Los Angeles never lacks unique luxury hotels. Here are the standout new hotels opened in the past three years, worth experiencing for something fresh. 01 Fairmont Century Plaza: A Legendary Hotel with Distinguished History The Fairmont Century Plaza was originally designed by renowned architect Minoru Yamasaki and opened in 1966 as a landmark of Los Angeles. It has been favored by multiple U.S. presidents and Hollywood celebrities, making it one of the most legendary hotels in American history. After being reimagined and renovated, the refreshed hotel reopened in 2019, where you can feel the blend of history and modern trends. The hotel is situated on a hilltop area at the intersection of East and West Hollywood, adjacent to Beverly Hills. Every room features a private terrace with unobstructed panoramic views from The Hollywood Hills to the Pacific Ocean. The rooftop pool serves as an excellent vantage point for enjoying Los Angeles 360 degrees around. In the ultra-luxury spa center spanning over 1,300 square meters, guests can also enjoy SPA treatments and Turkish baths.
