Los Angeles Vibrant Fun Guide #1: Dreamy and Thrilling Theme Parks

Los Angeles

Brief

Content

You can always believe in fairy tales, magic, and adventure. In Los Angeles, the birthplace of Hollywood, the world's largest film companies gather together - Universal, Warner, Paramount, Disney... All kinds of authentic movie-themed parks and studio tours are incredibly exciting. Time after time experiencing being immersed on-site, scene after scene with classics recreated before your eyes - Los Angeles theme parks are not just fun, they can also help you fulfill dreams! Hollywood Universal Studios ™: The first Universal Studio globally featuring an exclusive Studio Tour As the first Universal Studios worldwide, Hollywood Universal Studios ™ in Los Angeles offers many unique experiences. Besides classic attractions like Harry Potter's Wizarding World ™, Transformers, and Minions, it has recently added new IP experiences such as Secret Life of Pets and Kung Fu Panda. Universal Studios has upper and lower parks, connected by an escalator walkway. Apart from the three thrilling attractions - The Mummy, Transformers, and Jurassic Park - located in the lower park, all other rides are situated in the upper park. You can learn about locations, wait times, and more information for each attraction through Universal Studios app or paper maps with signs provided at the theme park. Must-visit Attractions Studio Tour: This one-hour Studio Tour is exclusive to Los Angeles and serves as a major attraction at Universal Studios Hollywood. You can ride on a sightseeing train, listen to the guide's commentary while passing through real movie production sets from Hollywood, personally experience simulated scenes from films like King Kong, Fast & Furious, Psycho, and Jaws, and witness the filming process behind special effects such as plane crashes, floods, and explosions. Tips: Participate in the Universal Studios Hollywood VIP Experience to ride a private tram through authentic studios featuring major motion pictures and television productions, explore the enormous prop warehouses of Universal Studios Hollywood, and glimpse areas not open to the general public. The Wizarding World of Harry Potter™: Experience the unparalleled "Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey," enjoy a Butterbeer, wave your wand at Ollivander's Wand Shop—it feels exactly like the magic world in the films. Waterworld: The park's most thrilling live show—you can watch an exciting water stunt performance. If you don't want to get wet, sit in the safe zone with silver chairs. Transformers™ Hub: Meet Optimus Prime and the Decepticons, and ride a 3D virtual roller coaster during "The Battle for the AllSpark" where you'll join the Transformers on a mission to save the city. Recently Added Attractions Secret Life of Pets: Escape Rope: A 2021 attraction where you can journey with adorable animal heroes through New York City streets to their new home. Kung Fu Panda: Dragon Warrior: A Chinese-themed ride opened in 2018, representing the first collaboration between Universal Studios and DreamWorks. It features innovative indoor projection mapping technology, offering a thrilling 180° realistic adventure experience. Attractions Not Yet Introduced at Beijing Universal Studios Besides the attractions already mentioned - The Simpsons' RoadTrip, Secret Life of Pets, Walking Dead Haunted House in upper park, and the Mummy ride in lower park - these are major IPs that Universal Studios Beijing does not have yet; additionally, while Jurassic World and Kung Fu Panda parks exist at Hollywood Studios, they differ from the flagship attractions there - you must experience those in Los Angeles! Jurassic Park River Adventure: Encounter lifelike dinosaurs while rafting through the jungle, feeling as if inside the movie. The final thrilling drop provides an exhilarating experience completely different from what Beijing offers. The Mummy Revenge Roller Coaster: Indoor high-speed roller coaster where your journey feels like exploring ancient Egyptian tombs, spent entirely in screams of excitement. One-Day Tour Recommendation If you purchase a regular single-day ticket, naturally you should first visit the most popular attractions with long queue times. If you have purchased Universal Express (queue priority) or VIP tickets (skip-the-line), then you can arrange your itinerary according to your preferences. Recommended one-day itinerary as follows: Movie Studios Tour → Upper Park (Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey → The Simpsons Ride → Despicable Me Minion Coaster → The Secret Life of Pets: Escape From the City → Kung Fu Panda: The Dragon Scroll → The Lost World: Jurassic Park) → Lower Park (Transformers 3D Virtual Roller Coaster → The Mummy 3D Roller Coaster → Jurassic Park River Adventure) → Back to Upper Park, explore Universal CityWalk, and purchase souvenirs. Compared to other Universal Studios parks, Los Angeles has a more authentic amusement atmosphere. Besides exciting and cutting-edge attractions, the park's dining options and souvenirs are constantly updated and refreshed. You can go to the newly opened Minion Café for unique desserts, or buy globally exclusive designs at souvenir shops. Official Website: https://www.universalstudioshollywood.com/ Universal Studios Hollywood Located in prime real estate in Los Angeles, originally a film studio for Hollywood movies, now transformed into a theme park. Combines Hollywood films with advanced special effects to create world-class amusement rides featuring cutting-edge technology. Jurassic Park, The Mummy Revenge roller coaster, Transformers, Minions and other 4D ride experiences transport visitors into virtual space-time, experiencing thrilling adventures until returning to reality only after the show ends. The most distinctive feature is the Studio Tour, where observation cars take visitors through actual Hollywood film sets, personally witnessing the filming of special scenes like plane crashes, floods, explosions, etc. The park features various shows daily, opportunities to pose for photos with ubiquitous Hollywood characters, and purchase popular merchandise, making it possible to spend an extraordinary day at Universal Studios. Warner Bros. Hollywood Studio Tour: A Journey Through Classic American TV Series Located in Burbank, Warner Bros. Studio is a genuine film and television production facility where you can bring your passion for Warner Bros. works, follow their Hollywood Studio Tour to travel through classic shows like "The Big Bang Theory," "Friends," "Batman," etc., exploring the behind-the-scenes of familiar scenes. Warner Bros. Hollywood Studio Tour offers four options: Studio Tour, Classic Tour, Deluxe Studio Tour Upgrade, and Ultimate Tour. All include guided commentary plus free exploration time. Guided tours primarily focus on visiting studios and exterior sets, while self-guided visits allow you to take photos at interior scenes, souvenir shops, and restaurants, and experience dining. 3-Hour Studio Tour: Includes 1 hour of guided commentary and 2 hours of free time. First, ride the sightseeing tram with your driver-guide, listening to explanations while visiting numerous studios and exterior sets. You'll discover many familiar scenes: The alley where Spider-Man and his love interest kiss upside down in the first movie, Sheldon's couch from The Big Bang Theory, the piano at Rick's Café from Casablanca, the coffee shop where Mia and Sean met in La La Land, Central Perk - the coffee house where friends gather in Friends... During your subsequent free time, you can take photos on the sofas of Friends' and The Big Bang Theory's apartments, try on the Hogwarts Sorting Hat, view Batman's Batmobile and Wonder Woman's costume... Finally, don't forget to purchase souvenirs for your favorite IP. 3-Hour Classic Tour: Designed specifically for nostalgic film fans, this tour focuses on classic films from the studio's founding through the 1970s. Visit historic sound stages and explore the Warner Bros Archives to see Ingrid Bergman's costumes from Casablanca. 4-Hour Studio Tour Upgrade: Features an additional hour of commentary compared to the regular tour. Your guide will take you extra exploring the historically significant Stage 110 acre studio, demonstrating the "magic" behind film and television production, with lunch at Friends Café included. 5-Hour Premium Tour: Designed for Hardcore Film and TV Fans This exclusive tour includes all the above experiences plus additional behind-the-scenes access, including a visit to the props warehouse where you can view rare artifacts from Hollywood's Golden Age, followed by a fine lunch at a high-end restaurant on the studio lot. ▶ Practical Information Address: 3400 W. Six Flags Magic Mountain: Theme Park with the Most Roller Coasters in the World Already tired of regular roller coasters and looking for something more thrilling? You absolutely must not miss Six Flags Magic Mountain, the theme park with the most roller coasters in the world. With its impressive 20 roller coasters, it holds the Guinness World Record. Whether you're sitting, lying down, kneeling, hanging, or standing, all types of roller coasters can be experienced here at once! Braves of all ages can find their thrill here: Adults can try Wonder Woman, the world's tallest and longest monorail roller coaster, newly launched in July 2022; Twisted Colossus, the longest speed-style steel-wood hybrid structure roller coaster on Earth; SUPERMAN: Escape from Krypton, which gives you a sensation of flying up like Superman; Tatsu, soaring above the park; Full Throttle, the world's tallest and fastest loop roller coaster; Apocalypse, an all-wooden roller coaster, and more. Families with children can ride Road Runner Express through the "desert," or head to Daffy's Adventure Tours for a magical tour bus journey suitable for younger guests. Retro enthusiasts should not miss Gold Rusher, an old mine train opened in 1971; The New Revolution – Classic, the world's first loop roller coaster that operated starting in 1976; World's first 4D roller coaster X2 and others not only have a very American style but are also the predecessors of various types of roller coasters. Besides roller coasters, there are all kinds of scream machines here that ignite the blood of lovers of weightlessness - 125-meter high drop tower, giant pendulum swing over 50 meters high... absolutely makes your adrenaline surge! ▶ Practical Information Address: 26101 Magic Mountain Pkwy, Valencia CA 91355 Getting There: Self-driving is recommended. You can also take Metro Antelope Valley Line to Newhall and then take a taxi. Tickets: Starting at $75 per day, available for purchase through the official website. Official Website: https://www.sixflags.com/magicmountain Six Flags Magic Mountain Located in northern Los Angeles, Six Flags Magic Mountain is simply a great holy land for coaster enthusiasts. With nearly 20 roller coasters, it claims the world's most awards for largest roller coaster amusement parks. Walking through the park, screams echo everywhere, with squeaking sounds between tracks and wheels. Every facility challenges people's adrenaline and stimulates human limits of endurance. The world's first 4D roller coaster Green Lantern (Green Lantern: First Flight), nearly 5 kilometers long America's longest roller coaster Goliath, Drop of Doom - the global second-tallest drop tower reaching 126 meters height, along with various scream machines bring visitors an unprecedented weightlessness experience, as if about to escape Earth's gravity. Even after the game ends, people remain calmless for a long time. Paradox Pictures Studio Tour: A Journey Through Iconic Sets and Prop Storage As a century-old powerhouse of Hollywood, Paramount Pictures has been operating for 110 years. Famous worldwide for its iconic opening logo featuring stars circling snow-capped mountains, Paramount dominated with classic films like The Godfather, Forrest Gump, Titanic, and Mission: Impossible. To satisfy your Paramount obsession and witness firsthand the legendary stories created by this century-old Hollywood brand, you must not miss their studio tours. They offer a 2-hour general tour and a 4.5-hour VIP experience that will fulfill all your expectations about Paramount. For the 2-hour studio tour, you'll be led by guides on a sightseeing vehicle exploring Paramount Studios grounds, including soundstages for Dr. Phil, street scenes used in Spider-Man, Indiana Jones, and Captain America films, water pools where special effects were filmed, and extensive prop warehouses filled with treasures from decades of cinema history. Throughout the tour, you'll gain deeper insights into Paramount's legacy. VIP tour registration grants additional privileges: personally experience props and sets from classic films while enjoying delicious snacks, uncovering more industry secrets, understanding Hollywood's inner workings, and hearing fascinating stories that took place at Paramount. Practical Information: Address: 5515 Melrose Ave Los Angeles CA 90038 Transportation: Accessible via the Metro Hollywood/Wilshire Line Hours of Operation: 9:00 AM - 3:30 PM Tickets: Studio tour $63, VIP tour $199. Booking must be done through the official website. Recommended for children aged 10 and above. Official Website: https://www.paramountstudiotour.com/ Paramount Studios Built in 1912, Paramount Studios is a film production site. The architecture here exudes strong local cultural atmosphere. Not only can you appreciate scenes from movies here, but you can also learn about the filmmaking process, which is truly eye-opening. Before leaving, why not purchase some movie-related items at the gift shop here, such as hats, T-shirts, business cards and other souvenirs. California Disneyland: The First Disney Resort with Dual Park Experience Located in Anaheim, California Disneyland Resort is only a 1-hour drive from Los Angeles. It consists of two super popular theme parks, so you can choose one for a Disney fairytale day trip. Disneyland Park was built in 1955 and is the world's first Disney theme park. Here not only are there signature Disney attractions, but with the development of Disney films, new elements such as Star Wars, Finding Nemo, and Avengers have been incorporated, giving this park a dual experience of both "classic" and "novelty". Disney California Adventure Park, located adjacent to Disneyland, opened as a brand new attraction in 2001, incorporating Californian style and architectural landmarks. This is a park designed for "grown-ups," featuring the longest-launch roller coaster track among all Disney parks worldwide, the Guardians of the Galaxy themed drop tower, one of only a handful of swinging Ferris wheels globally... Numerous innovative thrilling attractions let you enjoy everything in one visit. ▶ Practical Information Address: Disneyland Dr Anaheim CA 92802 USA Transportation: Recommended to drive. You can also take the 460 Metro Express Line bus directly from Figueroa St. in downtown Los Angeles, about 2 hours. Tickets: Available as single-park tickets and one-day two-park passes, purchasable through the Disneyland APP Website: https://disneyland.disney.go.com/ California Disneyland • Personally created by Disney's founder in California - the world's first Disney park, featuring highlights from all global Disney parks. • Composed of two attractions: Disneyland Park and Disney California Adventure Park. You can purchase admission to one park or get a comprehensive pass for both. ·The park is divided into five themed areas, including Main Street U.S.A., Adventureland, Frontierland, Fantasyland, and Tomorrowland. ·After sunset, spectacular fireworks are launched in front of the main castle; it's not just a paradise for children but also an entertainment destination for adults.
