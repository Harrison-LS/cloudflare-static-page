NFL Football showdown approaching – check out these fantastic sports bars in California

Los Angeles

Brief

Content

Sunday noon Pacific Time this week, the LA Rams—the beloved of Los Angeles—will face the number one seeded New Orleans Saints in the NFL Divisional Round. The winner will advance to the Super Bowl Championship on February 3rd. Despite being at a disadvantage due to head-to-head records, and having the game scheduled in New Orleans as their home field, we can still cheer for our city heroes through TV screens. With kickoff at 12:05 PM—right during lunch time—I'll recommend some great places perfect for friends gathering to eat, watch games, and cheer together! 01 Boiling Crab The chain seafood restaurant Boiling Crab has made quite a name for itself in Los Angeles. Here you can find all kinds of delicious crabs and lobster, along with Cajun-style corn and potatoes. When you come here, your mouth and hands will definitely not be idle. Even better, almost every location features multiple large-screen TVs, making it an ideal spot to eat while watching the game. California Pizza Kitchen California Pizza Kitchen, abbreviated as CPK, is also a renowned chain pizza restaurant in California. It features an innovative open kitchen concept that blends flavors from around the world, incorporating fresh ingredients and exotic dishes into pizzas. The thin crusts are crispy and delicious. Similarly, TV screens adorn the walls throughout the restaurants, allowing fans to cheer for their beloved teams while enjoying great food. Buffalo Wild Wings Buffalo Wild Wings, with its American bar-style ambiance, is undoubtedly a holy ground for sports fans in their hearts. During NFL, NBA, MLB, and important football games, this place is always bustling with people, and long lines form outside the doors. Besides its signature various flavored wings, served with or without bones, the beer here is also excellent. BrewCo Manhattan Beach This location offers fantastic beers and hamburgers, making it a top choice for sports fans. During football season, every weekend from 10am to 2pm, they offer an incredibly value Brunch featuring bottomless mimosas. And here we are near beautiful Manhattan Beach. After watching a game, you can stroll by the seaside and enjoy the long-awaited California sunshine, dispelling the gloom from these days of continuous rain - isn't that wonderful?
