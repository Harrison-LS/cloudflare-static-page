1. Why Choose Universal Studios Hollywood? America has two Universal Studios - one in Orlando and another in Los Angeles. Orlando's Universal Studio is the largest with newer facilities. However, when it comes to classics, Los Angeles Universal holds its own reputation. Universal Studios Hollywood is the world's first Universal Studio and also a filming location for many major Hollywood blockbusters like King Kong, Jurassic Park, Jaws, Fast & Furious, Desperate Housewives, Back to the Future, Vacation, etc. The super realistic special effects combined with thrilling rides create a theme park that recreates movie scenes. You can even encounter many famous stars at Universal Studios: the Beckham family, Jay Chou and Lin Qiuling, JJ Lin, Xenia Shu and Zhang Jie, and many more celebrities are all loyal fans of Universal Studios! Tips: Important Reminders! There is no same-day re-entry now. You can only enter once per day - once you exit, you cannot return! This rule applies to both groups and FIT travelers. So friends who plan to enter in the morning and play around: bring lunch or prepare to eat inside the park. You cannot go out, finish eating, then come back. Hollywood Universal Studios · Located in Los Angeles where land is extremely valuable, originally a Hollywood film production studio, now transformed into a theme park. · Universal Studios Hollywood combines famous films with advanced special effects to create world-class amusement facilities featuring cutting-edge technology. · Attractions like Jurassic Park, Revenge of the Mummy roller coaster, Transformers, and Minions 4D experiences transport visitors into virtual worlds where they can experience thrilling adventures before returning to reality once each ride concludes. · The most distinctive attraction is the Studio Tour, which takes visitors through actual Hollywood film sets via an observation vehicle, allowing them to witness firsthand how special scenes like plane crashes, floods, and explosions are filmed. · Inside the park, various shows are performed daily. Visitors can take photos with Hollywood characters found everywhere, purchase popular merchandise, and spend an extraordinary day at Universal Studios. 2. (Introduction to ticket types and pricing) Universal Studios Hollywood offers diverse tickets, including one-day passes, two-day passes, and annual passes; There are also priority and VIP tickets that allow skipping the queue. [Regular Tickets] 1-day pass, 2-day pass, 1-year pass. 1-day pass: Prices vary by date (peak vs off-peak season) at $116, $110, and $105. To find out what price applies on any specific day, you need to check the official website. The 1-day pass offers entry before 10 AM (which means entering one hour early), versus anytime throughout the day, with different prices for each option. (Seasons in America differ from China. The cheapest annual pass at $129 allows use on only 175 days, making weekends and holidays during peak season largely unusable. The most expensive 365-day usable pass costs $598 and is generally provided for local tour guides. [PRIORITY TICKETS: FRONT OF LINE] Priority tickets allow skipping the queue to directly ride attractions. Visitors can select this ticket type when purchasing. Upon entry, you receive a wristband or badge, which allows access through the fast-track lane without waiting in line. It's important to note that each attraction can only be accessed via priority once; staff will keep records, and for repeat rides, you'll need to join the regular queue. Priority ticket prices vary by date, ranging from $179 to $254. Specific prices should be checked on the official website. You can book tickets online in advance or purchase them after arriving at the park. However, tickets have a daily limit and sell out completely when exhausted. Tips: During the 2019 Spring Festival period (January 22 - February 19, 2019), Fast Pass offered a special promotion price of $159, but this was only available to travel agencies and could not be booked through the official website. Group package VIP service includes all above items plus personalized customization options. Whenever conditions allow, guest requests will be accommodated. By car: Universal Studios is located beneath the Hollywood 101 freeway, approximately ten minutes north of downtown Los Angeles. Google Maps navigation: You can directly search "Universal Studios Hollywood"; GPS device: Enter area code and specific address "00 Universal City Plaza, Universal City, CA 916081"; or GPS coordinates: 34.135832,-118.351166. There will be park signs along the road after exiting the freeway. Parking fees: Before 15:00 - $15; 15:00 until closing time - $10; Large tour vehicles - $17. Public transportation: Take the METRO Red Line subway and get off at Universal City Station, then cross the street to catch the free shuttle bus, which arrives at Universal Studios in just 3 minutes. 5. Attractions Universal Studios is divided into Upper and Lower Parks. The two parks are connected by an escalator passage. Upon entry, staff provides a map to each visitor which includes a Chinese version. The attractions include: 1) King Kong 360-Degree 3D Adventure; 2) The Mummy Revenge Roller Coaster; 3) Jurassic Park Rapids Ride; 4) Despicable Me Minions 3D Coaster; 5) Walking Dead Haunted House; 6) Water World; 7) Family Fun Coaster; 8) Harry Potter's Wizarding World; 9) Studio Tour; 10) Simpsons Virtual Roller Coaster; 11) Universal Studios Animal Actors Stage. (Attractions 1, 2, and 3 are in the Lower Park; all others are in the Upper Park) Additionally, each attraction has an electronic screen displaying the estimated queue time. Actual wait times tend to be 10-20 minutes longer than shown. 6. How to Experience the 6 Must-Ride Attractions Within 4 Hours Universal Studios Lower Park Step one: The park entrance is located in the Upper Park. It is recommended to take the escalator directly to the Lower Park first and enjoy the three attractions there. Lower Park riding order: Transformers 3D Coaster, Jurassic Park Rapids Ride, Mummy Revenge 3D Coaster. Must-ride attractions: Transformers 3D Virtual Coaster - Very popular, simulates completing missions with Transformers! Uses the latest 3D technology and top-tier visual effects; the screen is almost unnoticeable. Characters in the show move seamlessly between the surrounding environment and media. Jurassic Park Rapids Adventure - Recommended to sit in the second row; those in the first row will get completely soaked. Combines scenes from the "Jurassic Park" movie with a rapids ride, allowing you to see dinosaurs in their jungle habitat. You'll be chased by a 50-foot T-Rex, followed immediately by an exciting 84-foot drop! Rideable attractions: Mummy's Revenge 3D Virtual Coaster - More terrifying than Transformers, everything around is pitch black with very little light, and scary music continuously plays from speakers. Extremely thrilling! Universal Studios Upper Park - Step 2: Take the escalator from Lower Park back to Upper Park. Upper Park ride sequence: Harry Potter's Wizarding World, Universal Studio Tour, The Simpsons Virtual Coaster, Animal Actors Show, Despicable Me Minions 3D Virtual Coaster, The Walking Dead Haunted House, Water World, Family Fun Roller Coaster. Must-ride attractions: Harry Potter's Wizarding World - Walk into Hogwarts Castle and watch characters in the murals come to life, candles floating in mid-air, Principal Dumbledore's office, the Dark Arts Defense Classroom, Gryffindor's Common Room, and even see the Chamber of Secrets... Everything is exactly like the real magical world from the movies; Later is Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey—wearing 3D glasses on a live roller coaster ride, traveling through Hogwarts corridors with teachers alongside Harry Potter and his friends, soaring up and down within the castle for an extremely thrilling adventure. Studio Tour Take the tram into the film production base, lasting approximately 60 minutes. First you see large sound stages and studios, then enter simulated city streets such as New York Avenue and City Hall. Next comes a 4D movie theater where a golden sword fights dinosaurs—making it feel like dinosaurs are darting beside and above your vehicle, shaking the car or dropping it... Following that are disaster scenes including floods, subway earthquakes, and colliding vehicles, all incredibly realistic. Finally you enter the shooting locations of Jaws, Desperate Housewives, and the Boeing 747 crash site—everything feels so real and immersive, with one wave after another, overwhelming to take in. The Studio Tour is offered daily in Chinese, but times vary and guides are from different nationalities—you might even encounter a Thai guide speaking Chinese. Specific schedules can be checked on the official website. Walking Dead Haunted House Proceed with caution if you're timid! This haunted house combines special effects, realistic sets, electronic animation, and live performances—realistic zombies pour out! Children, elderly visitors, and pregnant women are advised not to participate. Water World Experience outdoor stage shows featuring water stunts and explosive effects. After entering, you can choose between green seats and silver seats. The green seats belong to the Splash Zone, which means guests will get intentionally splashed with water by staff members, leaving them very wet indeed; the silver seats represent the safe zone where you won't get wet. Universal Studios Top Family-Friendly Attractions Flight of the Hippogriff: When traveling with children, you can also ride "Flight of the Hippogriff" in The Wizarding World of Harry Potter! This is Hollywood Universal's first outdoor family roller coaster. You have the option to experience riding a Hippogriff on a magical journey. Soar above the pumpkin patch, dive downward, then pass through Hagrid's cottage—children will absolutely love it! Despicable Me & Simpsons Virtual Coaster: Both of these are 3D simulation coasters that use American animated characters to showcase film effects, creating the feeling of traveling through animation movies. Although the 3D coaster doesn't move much, only moving back and forth left-to-right, it still delivers sensations of weightlessness and bumps like a real roller coaster. The visual effects are truly impressive and perfect for children. Studio Animal Stage: Get close-up encounters with celebrity animals from films and learn how they receive training and perform alongside the world's most famous film stars. You might even have the chance to walk on stage and help train these animals. Additionally, there are special effects theaters, DinoLand USA, family-friendly roller coasters, and Shrek 4D Theater—all attractions suitable for families. 7. Women with medium-to-long hair please prepare elastic bands for tying your hair, especially before roller coasters. 5. If you're concerned about getting tanned, apply sunscreen in advance. 6. Visitors wearing glasses are advised to keep them secure while on the Jurassic Adventure ride. 8. Each attraction offers single rider lanes with shorter wait times than standard queues. To save time, consider separating from your companions to ride individually. 10.
