Ride the World's Shortest Railway Where is the world's shortest railway? The answer lies in Los Angeles. Angels Flight Railway (天使铁路) in Los Angeles spans just 99 meters, costing only $1 to ride the entire route. Built in 1901, it was originally designed to help residents living higher up reach the market area more quickly, replacing the previous staircase of over 200 steps.
