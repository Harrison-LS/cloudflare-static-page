About Ice Cream Museum: As a famous influencer museum that opens only for limited time every summer, the Ice Cream Museum has always been New Yorkers exclusive privilege. Finally, in the scorching heat of 2017, friends in Los Angeles got a chance to see the charm of ice cream right at their doorstep! Ticket sales started with Amex Platinum members advance sale on March 30th. Within less than a week from the start, all weekend tickets were snapped up. As of now, all tickets have been sold out. Friends who haven't had a chance to visit can only wait until next year~ But you can make up for this small regret here first Pink themed official website: *** Museum is located in L.A. All displays are not real food~ Basically every room has staff distributing tasting samples The first room upon entering features dessert-decorated walls and a Dove chocolate cart. The second room has about a dozen pink phones you can pick up to hear introductions and instructions. Behind this pink door lies an entirely new world. A passionate tropical atmosphere greets you immediately. The ice cream from Santa Barbara given in the first room tastes the best throughout the entire venue - definitely worth getting more of.
