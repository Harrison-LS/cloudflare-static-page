There will be fewer people and more parking spaces available, making it easier to park. ◎ Parking My usual habit is to first enter from West Village Entrance 3 to find a parking spot, since I typically start exploring from that side. However, if you can't find parking in this area at all, or after you've finished browsing here and are too tired to walk from West Village to East Village (trust me, your feet will get sore easily after a day of shopping!), I'll drive directly to the Parking Garage in East Village to park before continuing. This allows you to: 1) put previously purchased items into the car to reduce carried items and weight; 2) conserve some walking energy for further exploration. However, this area is very dry throughout the year, especially hot in summer, so be sure to keep water with you at all times. ◎ Clothing The desert location creates significant temperature variations—extremely hot during the day but noticeably cooler in the evening. It's essential to dress appropriately and follow a layering approach for maximum comfort across different conditions. ◎ Coupon Book Just register online as a member and you can download exclusive discount coupons from the official website (available in Simplified Chinese!) for free to get your coupon book!
