Guide to World-Famous Instagram Walls | Los Angeles' "Love Wall"—Born by Coincidence, Yet Unexpectedly Went Viral

Los Angeles

Brief

A few spray paint cans of different colors, combined with simple heart elements, accidentally created an Instagram-famous influencer wall.

Content

On Valentine's Day 2015, a graffiti wall covered in hearts appeared outside L'asso restaurant on the streets of Los Angeles. Even its creator James Goldcrown couldn't have predicted that overnight this wall's image would become wildly popular on the social media platform Instagram. Hearts in various colors seemed to encapsulate different emotions within love: red represents passion, yellow conveys bitterness, and blue expresses melancholy... Although "Love Wall" has spawned series works with other background color schemes, the most representative remains always this Los Angeles Love Wall with white as its base color. Consequently, many influencers came seeking fame by taking photos in front of this artwork. Today, the wall outside L'asso has been replaced with other paintings, but this event will recreate this love wall at Taikoo Li in Chengdu. How popular is the Love Wall? James once said in an interview, "I saw friends from all over the world reposting images of the Love Wall online. There were Italian, Korean, Japanese... Although I don't understand these letters, I know that love is a lesson we share together. This language requires no explanation." It is precisely because of the strong support from netizens that James now dedicates himself to "transporting the Love Wall," spreading love elements to more cities. You can easily find this wall's presence in places like New York, Miami, Brazil, and Argentina. Even many hot-selling brands have adopted the heart motif as the ultimate fashion trend symbol. For example, popular cosmetics brand Maybelline incorporated these hearts into their products, making them highly eye-catching and trendy. However, its creation was actually a coincidence. In fact, the Love Wall began on a door: When James was testing spray paint can pressure, he casually drew colorful heart symbols on his studio's door. A customer who came to pick up an ordered painting took a liking to this door and purchased it. Afterward, James created paintings featuring these "heart" elements. At the Basel exhibition, collectors quickly acquired them. It was then that he realized the simplest hearts hit people most directly, until L'asso's owner allowed him to paint on their restaurant wall, giving birth to the "Love Wall" that went viral on Instagram. Later it took new forms. Brimming with creativity, James created many derivative street art pieces based on the Love Wall. New York | "Kids in Love" This work is not only a New York street mural but also scrolls back and forth across Times Square's giant screens. Everyone has different thoughts about it, which also includes quite a bit of philosophy about love, such as "we are all children in love" ... New York | "Not Just Black or White" James no longer confines his works solely to the topic of love; he uses them to reveal deeper truths. In this painting, the classic black and white colors highlight a single red heart. James wants to express that everything is not limited to two sides—black or white—and encourages people to dare to pursue and innovate boldly. How to Take Photos Use eye-catching hearts as the background, pair with a simple and elegant solid-color garment, and you already have enough to create an attention-grabbing photo. The article provides the following tips for reference; master these techniques to make your photos more unique! Reject "Standing Stiffly" Posing is always a major challenge for those who love taking pictures. Standing in front of the love wall and painstakingly trying various poses actually isn't as good as getting yourself moving—whether it's running, jumping, drinking coffee, or talking on the phone, your photos will appear more dynamic and natural. Use Small Props Besides putting extra effort into your outfit, small props can also be another useful tool for photography. For example, you could use sunglasses reflections to frame the love wall in your shot. Take someone you like and shoot together Compared to solo photos, this wall might be more suitable for taking group photos with the person you like. Nowadays, the meaning of the love wall points not only directly to "love" but can also represent parental love and sisterhood. So whether it's close friends or parents and partners, they are all the best candidates to leave their images together in front of the love wall. Conclusion The birthplace of this love wall, Los Angeles, has always been a trendy city favored by artists. Therefore, street graffiti has become one major attraction of the city. If you plan a trip to Los Angeles, visiting these popular photo spots on the streets is also an interesting travel experience.
