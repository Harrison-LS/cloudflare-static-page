First shopping stop: Rodeo Drive Rodeo Drive • Known as the "celebrity wardrobe," Rodeo Drive is Los Angeles' top-tier shopping street and one of the favorite shopping destinations for celebrities. • Here you can find the latest branded items in the world, with international luxury names all available such as Giorgio Armani, Chanel, Dolce & Gabbana, and more. • Each luxury brand has its own exclusive villa with unique design styles, creating a distinctive landscape along Rodeo Drive. • Beyond shops, there are upscale restaurants, bars, galleries, and other venues, giving you both culinary satisfaction and visual enjoyment while shopping. Rodeo Drive is located in Beverly Hills, Los Angeles. Magnificent storefronts line both sides of the avenue. The store decorations are luxurious with artistic features. Being surrounded by them feels like being inside an art museum. Rodeo Drive gathers jewelry and fashion design works from internationally renowned master designers most loved by the public: Giorgio Armani (Giorgio Armani), Chanel, Dolce & Gabbana, Badgley Mischka (Badgley Mischka) For this reason, Rodeo Drive is hailed as "one of the world's top ten shopping paradises." Worth noting: Brand stores on Rodeo Drive close relatively early - at 5:30 PM. So please plan your time accordingly based on your schedule.
