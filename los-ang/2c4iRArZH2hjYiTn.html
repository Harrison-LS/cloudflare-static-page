Los Angeles does have a world-famous Paul Smith pink wall that ignites your girly heart, but with more and more people queuing up to check in and worship at it, you always feel that Moments and Instagram are becoming increasingly similar! But actually in this beautiful City of Angels, there are countless niche pink photo spots that are even more popular than the Pink Wall - ice cream shops, cafes, hotels, art museums. Who isn't a little princess who loves pink? The Beverly Hills Hotel Location: 9641 Sunset Blvd, Beverly Hills, CA 90210 Marilyn Monroe once stayed here for three months. The hotel has an incredibly girly overall design with pale pink and light green interwoven, and anything you casually take a picture of becomes a big shot! The Beverly Hills Hotel known as the "Pink Palace" isn't undeserved. Hotel walls, pool chairs, brunch tables... whenever you can think of decorations or architecture, they all use pink to complete them! Although a night's hotel room costs $500+ USD, but just coming here for brunch to check in and take photos, we earth-eating young girls can still afford it! Cabana Cafe at The Beverly Hills Hotel is the pink restaurant in the hotel! Open daily from 9 AM - 6 PM, recommended to make a reservation in advance and meet up with your best friends for a pink Girls' Brunch! The Hart and The Hunter Location: 7950 Melrose Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90046 This The Hart and The Hunter, located on Los Angeles' famous trendy street, is just a few stores away from a Paul Smith store. However, with its serious-looking brown wooden exterior, no one would guess that inside hides a completely pink bar area! The ceiling, floor, walls, doors, and all decorations are all shades of pink - a completely pink paradise! On happy days, order some non-alcoholic drinks, take photos in the little room - it's absolutely dreamy. ACE Gallery Los Angeles Location: 5514 Wilshire Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90036 If you like The Broad and LACMA, then Ace Gallery, renowned for modern art, will also captivate you. Like a hidden gem in Los Angeles' Koreatown, every piece at Ace Gallery is perfect for Instagram! 📷📷 This pink-lit wall is just the tip of the iceberg for this contemporary art museum, with many artworks shining brilliantly here 🎨🎨 Although I'm an art novice, I truly feel it's full of wonder everywhere, and everything is perfect for photos! Hauser & Wirth Location: 901 E 3rd St, Los Angeles, CA 90013 The Hauser & Wirth in Los Angeles' Art District is definitely art-related too! Founded by two Swiss people, they now have exhibition spaces in New York, Los Angeles, and London. The Los Angeles space feels like it's being completed in a factory, with exhibitions changing over time as they progress. I've heard Hauser & Wirth will soon open in Hong Kong as well. Among them are two rooms featuring Jason Rhoades' installations composed of pink walls and various chandeliers! Not only can you experience cultural impact through keywords hanging from the ceiling, but this place is also very pink and perfect for photos. Bumsan Organic Milk Bar Location: 534 S Western Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90020 As a newly opened ice cream shop that has already gained popularity all over LA, Bumsan Organic Milk Bar comes with an idol-like image. During the soft opening, Girls' Generation's Sunny came to support them, instantly creating a pink ice cream storm. The entire pink little house and four pink walls don't lose momentum to Paul Smith's famous influencer wall. As for how the 🍦 tastes, I'll leave you with a suspense! You can taste it and know haha haha Alfred Tea Room Location: 8428 Melrose Pl, Los Angeles, CA 90069 The fame of Alfred Tea Room doesn't really need much more introduction! I visited some pink-themed shops this March and shared my experience - the super pink storefronts and detailed decorations completely triggered girly feelings! Saying this shop has zero dead angles from every angle for photos isn't an exaggeration! The milk tea tastes great, the only downside is the queue takes a bit longer. CREME Location: 3726 W 6th St, Los Angeles, CA 90005 I highly recommend this ice cream shop hidden in a corner of Koreatown! The ice cream has a rich creamy texture - both tiramisu and matcha flavors are delicious! My favorite is the homemade cone - it feels exactly like an egg waffle when you bite into it 🍦 Taking photos with popsicle sticks against the pink wall makes girly feelings overflow even more. Spending some time here chatting with friends creates a really pleasant afternoon. Actually, there are still many more pink influencer-worthy shops I haven't had time to introduce, but I believe these pink photo spots should be enough for everyone to enjoy exploring for weeks!
