LA Art Show | Los Angeles Art Exhibition LA Art Show The grandest art exhibition in all of Los Angeles is the annual LA Art Show, where visitors can appreciate contemporary, modern, historical, and traditional artworks. Whether you know a little about art or not, I'm sure there will be pieces that move you here. Last year, the art show attracted nearly 70,000 participants, covering an area of 150,000 square feet. Single-day tickets are $30, four-day passes are $60, and VIP opening night tickets are $200. Specific schedule: January 23: 7-11pm January 24-26: 11am-7pm January 27: 11am-5pm Address: Los Angeles Convention Center 1201 South Figueroa Street, West Hall, CA, 90015 A Blessing for Photography Enthusiasts | Women of Vision | Limited-Time Photography Exhibition Forest Lawn Museum Forest Lawn Museum presents the Women of Vision limited-time photography exhibition, featuring nearly a hundred works from eleven award-winning National Geographic female photographers. This exhibition gives you a unique opportunity to discover the stories behind photographers' lenses and pay tribute to the pioneering female photographers whose vision and ambition have reshaped photography. The show will feature nearly 100 photographs, including evocative portraits of distant and unknown cultures; striking conceptual themes such as memory and adolescent brain chemistry; and powerful works addressing social issues like child marriage and contemporary slavery in the twenty-first century. Additionally, the exhibition reveals how National Geographic's photo editors collaborate closely with photographers throughout their creative process. On-site video clips filmed from a first-person perspective vividly highlight each photographer's unique style, passion, and masterful techniques. Date: Jan 09, 2019 – Apr 7, 2019 Address: 1712 S.
