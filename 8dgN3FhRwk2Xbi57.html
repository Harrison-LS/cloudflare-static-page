Los Angeles' incredibly delicious and budget-friendly eats that even Hollywood celebs love!

Los Angeles

Brief

Have you heard that eating out in America can be quite expensive? Just thinking about it hurts your wallet. But no matter how high the cost of living is in any city, there are always some affordable culinary gems hidden around every corner. After extensive searching, I've finally discovered a cluster of popular and budget-friendly restaurants in Los Angeles where you can enjoy delicious food at great prices. They say there's even a chance to run into celebrities! Food lovers, come on in and take a look!

Pink's Hot Dog - A Hot Dog That Attracts Countless Celebrities BY @SiShiSanZhuoHua When it comes to American cuisine, you must start with hot dogs. Let's get to know a hot dog that everyone loves, from Hollywood celebrities down to regular folks: Pink's Hot Dog. Opened in 1939 and still operating today, Pink's is absolutely the king of popularity among hot dog joints. Despite being a small street-side shop, it has attracted countless Hollywood stars including Jackson, Katy Perry, Mrs. Obama... too many famous people have visited, so I won't list them all - just go check out the photo wall yourself. Image by @ChloeAlwaysOnTheRoad Pink's Hot Dog portions are substantial and there are plenty of varieties to choose from. What makes it most special is that they add Mexican chili sauce and guacamole, creating a rich flavor experience. The hot dogs themselves are named after Hollywood movies or TV shows, giving the place an authentic Hollywood atmosphere. You never know which star you might encounter at Pink's! Philippe the Original - Classic French Dip Sandwich Founded in 1908, Philippe the Original is absolutely a century-old establishment that is well-known everywhere. After 1951, it relocated to next to Chinatown. It is said that The French Dip was invented here. Legend has it that when police officers came in to order, the owner accidentally dropped a piece of baguette into a dish full of dipping sauce. This fortunate coincidence led to the creation of this delicious food. It seems every food has an unexpected story behind it, such as Yunnan Bridge Noodle Soup. This classic French Dip Sandwich is made with cheese-filled French bread rolls stuffed with roasted beef, roasted pork, roasted lamb leg, turkey, or ham. You can choose single sauce, double sauce, or fully covered in sauce based on your preference for richness. It's recommended to try the mustard sauce - the flavor is truly excellent. Irv's Burgers - Affordable burgers you must eat in Los Angeles Irv's Burgers has been open since 1946, the most popular is the Old School burger. Friendly service and reasonable prices have made it stand firm for decades. Even with soaring rent, prices remain surprisingly affordable.
