Studio Tour The highlight of Universal Studios - a sneak peek behind the scenes of production! Comedic actors in unicycle costumes, host Jimmy Fallon will take you on a journey to see how various Hollywood blockbusters are actually made. Duration: 45 minutes, departing on the hour each day. The Mandarin version of the miniature train departs at :15 or :45 (***) #12). Features include a tour of classic film shooting locations and an up-close experience with "King Kong 360° 3D Adventure" by Peter Jackson - enjoying the world's most spectacular 3D immersion. See vehicles and filming special effects from "Fast & Furious" "Witness" a terrifying aerial disaster in World War II: The Boeing 747 crash Meet Norman Bates at the legendary Bates Motel featured in "Psycho" Gather courage to explore deep waters searching for traces of "Jaws" During an earthquake, experience subway car derailment and flooding - play the role of lead characters... WaterWorld Water World Experience the world's most exquisitely produced live water stunts and explosive performances. Watch as daredevil actors perform dangerous wavesurfing tricks alongside breathtaking explosion effects. (Note: These actors can be reckless. After entering, you'll notice some seats are colored blue - those blue seats will get splashed with water.) If you don't want to get wet, just sit a few rows further back.) Despite appearing as childish animation, the thrill of 🎢 is incredibly satisfying! Jurassic Park Rapids Ride Want to have fun at a theme park? How can you resist the thrill of getting soaked? Embark on an exciting and thrilling rafting adventure! You will definitely get wet with this ride. Inspired by the movie "Jurassic Park," you will see dinosaurs roaming freely in the jungle. While narrowly escaping a 50-foot tall Tyrannosaurus Rex on your raft, hold on tight! Next comes a heart-pounding 84-foot drop. Location: Lower Park Ride Requirements: Minimum height of 42 inches (107 cm). Children aged 42-48 inches (107-122 cm) must be accompanied by a guardian over the age of 14 to ride. Mummy Revenge Roller Coaster Honestly, when Bumble first rode it, she found it somewhat terrifying. Plus various scary insects attacking (air blasts directed at your feet), but honestly, it's just very scary sound effects and visuals. Definitely a spine-chilling roller coaster journey. Shrek 4-D Theater This has to be one of the calmest attractions in the park, right? The 4D film combines 3D visuals with seats that move so vigorously you feel like you're getting whiplash, plus water sprayed in your face from the row ahead. Join Shrek and Donkey as they venture into a fairy tale adventure world to rescue the princesses! At the start, guests will stand in the lobby viewing an animated segment. Don't rush—this is just the prelude. Hang in there until the lobby show ends, then you'll enter the actual theater for the true 4D motion picture experience. By the time this ride concludes, you're definitely exhausted! The longest part isn't actually riding—it's waiting. But that's simply how it works at America's entertainment capital—Universal Studios.
