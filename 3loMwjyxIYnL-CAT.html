Although you may be far from home, you must never take Spring Festival lightly! There are definitely activities not to miss~ The editor here has summarized Los Angeles 2019 Spring Festival activities for everyone so you can also feel a strong taste of traditional Chinese New Year in Los Angeles~ If your schedule is arranged well, you can visit various different styles of Spring Festival celebrations and experience the New Year atmosphere~ Experience the happiest New Year at the happiest place on Earth - Disneyland What could be more suitable than "the happiest place on earth" for celebrating Lunar New Year? This year Disney seems especially enthusiastic about Chinese New Year, with month-long activities to commemorate the Year of the Pig. Many Disney characters will wear traditional Chinese clothing to celebrate the arrival of Spring Festival. Essential elements like lanterns are scattered throughout Disneyland. It is particularly worth mentioning that the Three Little Pigs from Disney classic animation will also participate in parades to celebrate the Year of the Pig. Friends can take photos with Mulan, Mickey, and Minnie, as well as watch the parades. There are also delicious delicacies from China, Japan, Korea, and Vietnam throughout the park. The Chinatown Central Plaza New Year celebrations this year feature a wide variety of performances ranging from kung fu demonstrations to traditional music performances; there are also food trucks, face painting, storytelling, paper folding, and various other activities. But what attracts people most is the Golden Dragon Parade in the afternoon, starting at approximately 1pm. Those interested should not miss it!
