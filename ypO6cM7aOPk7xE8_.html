As is well known, LA is a pilgrimage site for all major influencers, featuring not only the self-trending influencer wall, but also various spots perfect for taking famous social media photos, restaurants, museums, desserts, attractions, and so on. About Influencer Streets Among all these influencer spots, my favorite is the street I'm introducing today - Abbot Kinney Blvd! A street worth spending a whole day exploring! Every store on this street is truly worth visiting - each one! It features mostly designer brands, and every shop's decoration style is very unique, exquisite, with incredible attention to detail that captivates you! The main area of Abbot Kinney Blvd worth exploring runs from Venice Blvd to Main Street. Influencer Store Recommendations 01.
